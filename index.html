About

I don't wait for the frontier, I build it. My work focuses on symbolic AI, quantum–classical computation, and theorem-proving infrastructures designed to tackle the hardest problems in mathematics and computation. The goal: turn speculative concepts into operational systems that scale across research, enterprise, and national-level applications.

Flagship Platforms

ProofX → A research-grade AI environment for frontier conjectures (Collatz, Riemann, Goldbach, Twin Primes). Its flagship engine, CollatzLab, pioneers hybrid quantum–classical exploration of stopping-time dynamics, enabling pattern discovery and conjecture synthesis at scales previously out of reach.

Philosophy

I operate as a systems architect, building frameworks that endure beyond hype cycles. Whether orchestrating multi-prover engines, synthesizing new conjectures, or bridging human–machine cognition, I thrive where theory is dense, stakes are high, and execution demands interdisciplinary fluency.

My mandate is impact over vanity: to engineer infrastructures that last, amplify discovery, and redefine the boundaries of proof and computation.
