Hi — I'm Alkindi.

I care about systems, engineering, and the disciplines that teach us how things hold together. My interests span computation, visual structure, and long-form thinking.

About

I grew up drawn to the inner workings of everyday systems — from simple tools to structured landscapes. That instinct later shaped my interests around computation, math, and the way ideas translate into real-world artifacts.

I try to keep my work grounded: learn deeply, build deliberately, and follow curiosity without theatrics. This site is not a portfolio as much as a quiet record of what I care about and where I'm spending time.
