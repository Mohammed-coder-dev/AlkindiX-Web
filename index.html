Hi — I’m Alkindi.

I explore systems — how ideas become structure; how structure becomes clarity. This space gathers the threads I follow: computation, photography, and slow thinking.

About

I grew up curious about how things fit together — mechanical tools, coded systems, structural landscapes. Over time that instinct evolved into an interest in computation, geometry, and the textures that sit beneath the surface of ordinary life.

I try to work with restraint: build quietly, refine deliberately, and take the time to understand what I’m touching. This site is a simple record — not a pitch — of where my attention goes.
