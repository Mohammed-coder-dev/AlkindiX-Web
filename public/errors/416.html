RANGE_NOT_SATISFIABLE

What happened?

Your request included a Range header asking for specific portions of a resource, but the server cannot fulfill that range request. This often happens with:

  • Invalid range specifications
  • Ranges beyond the resource size
  • Unsupported range units
  • Too many range requests
Related Range Errors:
RANGE_END_NOT_VALID, RANGE_GROUP_NOT_VALID, RANGE_MISSING_UNIT,
RANGE_START_NOT_VALID, RANGE_UNIT_NOT_SUPPORTED, TOO_MANY_RANGES
